# AgenticThat Publishing Companion privacy

The extension connects the AgenticThat publishing dashboard at
`https://agenticthat.netlify.app` to the AgenticThat Publishing Companion on
`http://127.0.0.1:8792`.

The extension does not collect, sell, transmit, or store social-network
passwords. Users enter social credentials directly on each social network's own
Chrome page. The extension transfers publishing requests and selected media
between the dashboard and the local companion on the same computer.

Publishing metadata, uploaded media, schedules, and saved browser sessions are
stored locally by the companion. They are not sent to AgenticThat by this
extension. The extension requests access only to the AgenticThat dashboard and
the local companion address required for this function.
